Lasagna

Description

This is a lovely, meat lovers lasagna that can satisfy the whole family

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Step to make:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the Lasagna
      5. Steps to layer
    1. Meat Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan Cheese
    7. Repeat step and then top with Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest and then serve
